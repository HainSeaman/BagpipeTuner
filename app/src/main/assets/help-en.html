Help

General

Why is no tone displayed, why doesn't the app respond?

  • The "Start Tuning" button must be active so that "Stop Tuning" is displayed.
  • The microphone permissions are missing. Please go to the app settings and grant the necessary permissions.

Why does the tuner on my smartphone show strange values?

  • The microphone of a smartphone is not designed as an instrument microphone—especially not for the very high and sharp tones of the pipes. It often cannot handle the mix and richness of overtones, making detection of the fundamental tone unreliable.
    In such cases, an external, higher-quality microphone can help. Most inexpensive microphones are overwhelmed by the pipes.
  • Keep at least one meter of distance between the chanter and the microphone; for the drones, the microphone can be placed closer.

Tips

  • Tune the chanter cleanly first before working on the drones.
  • Avoid background noise to improve tone detection.
  • Maintain a steady, consistent pressure when tuning.
  • For consistent measurements, use the app at a stable room temperature.
